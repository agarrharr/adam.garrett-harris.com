Plugins

I've made a couple of free plugins for Wordpress. Feel free to try them out and to leave feedback. Starbucks Reload- This plugin allows you to put a widget on your site that allows visitors to reload your Starbucks card. Google Bookshelves- This plugin allows you to put a widget on your site that automatically displays the books in your Google Books
